My Story

I began programming on and off sometime in early 2020, ever since then, I've always had a passion for writing software. Around a year and a half after high school I enrolled in the Winter 2022 semester of the Computer Engineering Technology Program at Algonquin College. It was there where I learned a variety of both practical programming classes and computer science theory. Fortunately I also was able to gain real world software development experience through my internship placement at Curtiss-Wright.

Writing software is interesting to me becuase I truly enjoy the craft of building a system from scratch, I like that no matter what I write I feel as though it can always be improved, like there's always something new to learn. An interest in learning is one of the main reasons I chose this carrer path in the first place, I've always really enjoyed learning new things.
